how does it work?

step

№1

goals & planing

It is all about understanding your goals in order to define the scope

A well documented plan will assure that goals become measurable and your investement in security awareness prove its value.

  • Understand your compliance requirements, available resources and budget restrictions. Define the roadmap and build the awareness plan.
  • Define target groups, required languages and other training requirements. Work out key indicators to measure the training sucess. Define training topics.

step

№2

assess the level of awareness

Use phishing simulations or assessments to understand the current awareness level

In order to improve the user beahviour we recommend measuring the current awareness level. This can be done through attack simulations or exams.

  • Perform phishing simulations with sample selections from different dixisions.
  • Create interactive exams where users can put the knowlege to the test.№ 3

3. run an awareness campaign

Improve the security awareness level with tailored trainings. Optimize the program over time

The awareness campaign is an ongoing process and involes more than just doing a few elearnings. It is a commitements to change the company culture.

  • Create role based awareness training for different divisions and measure progress. Use different tools like posters, videos and interactive elements to keep the user engaged.
  • Analyze results and adjust the training program. Assess the awareness level again with exams or attacks simulations and measure effectiveness of the program.
